ABOUT ME

I'm a third year student at Toronto Metropolitan University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Biology. I'm also a research assistant in the Laboratory for Translational Medicine, on the hunt to find the best combination of machine learning algorithms to perform medical imaging segmentation.

My favourite thing about Computer Science is how it uses creativity to solve complex problems, with endless opportunities to learn something new. I love a good challenge, because it means I'll never be bored! I have a keen interest in bioinformatics, and improving the quality and accessibility of healthcare worldwide.

I love cats, coffee/chai and sleeping in. When I'm not busy studying, you'll find me dabbling in a new sewing project, making a quilt, experimenting in the kitchen, or exploring new cuisines around the city!
